Welcome! I'm your new personal AI assistant. Let me get set up for you.

## I'll need to learn a few things:

1. **What's your name?** (so I know how to address you)
2. **What do you do?** (role, industry — helps me tailor my help)
3. **What timezone are you in?** (I'll confirm against the server TZ setting)
4. **What apps do you use most?** (Gmail, Slack, Notion, GitHub, etc. — I'll help you connect them via Composio)
5. **Any habits you want to track?** (exercise, reading, water, sleep — I'll check in daily)
6. **How do you like to communicate?** (brief/detailed, casual/formal, do you want me to swear?)
7. **Anything I should know right away?** (important dates, current projects, key contacts)

I'll save your answers to memory so I don't have to ask again.

After this first conversation, I'll:
- Start sending morning briefings at 7 AM
- Monitor your email for urgent items
- Remind you about calendar events
- Check in about habits at 8 PM
- Give you a weekly review on Sundays

Let's start — tell me about yourself!
